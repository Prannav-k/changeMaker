About Us

ChangeMaker is an initiative by Mohan Foundation,India. It is a bridge to connect healthcare professionals in the transplant field anywhere in the world with matching programs in India where they can make a difference.

MOHAN Foundation-india

MOHAN Foundation is a not-for-profit, non-governmental organization started to promote organ donation. It is a registered NGO with Income Tax exemption under Section 80G and 35AC and has offices in Chennai, Delhi-NCR, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Nagpur, Jaipur and Chandigarh with an information centre in the USA. It was started in 1997 in Chennai by philanthropists and medical professionals led by Dr. Sunil Shroff. The Government of India passed a law in 1994 to make it possible to donate organs in the event of brain death and stop commercial dealings in organs. Due to this law, it is now possible to not only donate oneâ€™s eyes, but also donate other vital organs such as the heart, lungs, liver, pancreas and kidneys. MOHAN Foundation through its work has helped improve organ donation in India and over the years impacted the lives of approximately 5500 patients who were suffering from vital organ failure to get a second chance in life through a transplant surgery and through tissue donations as well. The efforts of MOHAN Foundation have resulted in a direct increase in the national deceased organ donation rate from 0.16 per million population in 2012 to 0.34 per million population in 2014; an increase of more than 100% in two years.To learn more visit MOHAN Foundation or click here to see video

MOHAN USA

MOHAN USA, Inc. is a 501(c) (3) registered non-profit organization that was formed to increase awareness about organ shortage and promote transparency and ethical organ transplantation in India and the rest of the world. MOHAN USA was founded by a group in the USA that includes eminent doctors and individuals who are passionate about the cause of organ donation. To learn more about MOHAN USA click here.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring back best practices in the field of organ donation and transplantation to india.The visiting professionals,depending on thier availability, can contribute in a variety of ways such as delivering a lecture or helping or mentoring a transplant program

What we do

Mohan foundation has worked for last twenty years to improve the organ donation rate in india.We work with a number of hospitals in india to help with capacity building in the field

Our Commitment

Mohan Foundation is a non-profit Organisation that organises various aspects of given thoughts

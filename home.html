About Us

ChangeMaker is an initiative by Mohan Foundation,India. It is a bridge to connect healthcare professionals in the transplant field anywhere in the world with matching programs in India where they can make a difference.

Our Vision

Our mission is to bring back best practices in the field of organ donation and transplantation to india.The visiting professionals,depending on thier availability, can contribute in a variety of ways such as delivering a lecture or helping or mentoring a transplant program

What we do

Mohan foundation has worked for last twenty years to improve the organ donation rate in india.We work with a number of hospitals in india to help with capacity building in the field
